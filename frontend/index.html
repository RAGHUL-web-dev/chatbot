Jesuit heritage · est. 1925 · Centenary 2026

Founded by the Society of Jesus (Jesuits) in 1925, Loyola College has shaped generations with a motto “Lucet et Ardet” (Light and Warmth). 2025-26 marks the centenary year. Notable alumni: Nobel laureate Sir C.V. Raman, actors Vijay, Suriya, Vikram, director Mani Ratnam, cricketer R. Ashwin.
